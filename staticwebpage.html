Stand-up comedy


Stand-up comedy is a comedy performance to a live audience, addressed directly from the stage. The performer is known as a comic, comedian, or simply stand-up. Stand-up comedy consists of one-liners, stories or observations that may incorporate props, music or magic tricks. Stand-up comedy can be performed almost anywhere, including but not limited to clubs, festivals, bars, nightclubs, colleges or theatres. Stand-up comedy is the art of telling funny jokes. An unfunny joke is still quite a joke. It’s an attempted funny joke. people who are stressed out or just want to have some time pass or to relax for one or other means people are looking forward to watch standup comedy it may be online or offline.
